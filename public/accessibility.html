Accessibility Statement

VillageVibe is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

  • Use of semantic HTML and ARIA attributes
  • Keyboard navigability for all interactive elements
  • Alt text for all meaningful images
  • Color contrast that meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards
  • Continuous accessibility testing and improvement

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions, please contact us at hello@villagevibe.in.

Ongoing Efforts

We regularly review our website to ensure accessibility and welcome feedback from our users to help us improve.
